A ship in the ocean. Sailing the net, passing unseen in the waves.

A website of bits and bobs that I felt the need to cast into the world wide web.


For now the most active page is Links. This is my directory of interesting sites, accumulated documents, and a place to keep my precious collection of links. These are usually resources related to the projects I'm working on or curious oddities that I've stumbled upon during my visits to the quieter sides of the net.


Check out Perlinfor your very own randomized perlin noise sample. You can keep reloading the page until you find something nice. All rendered in plain text so you can paste it wherever you like.


Archiveis a playlist of some great classic rock. I used https://watsonbox.github.io/exportify/to export my Spotify playlist as csv and then https://www.convertcsv.com/csv-to-html.htm to convert the csv to an html table. Sometime down the line I'd like to add this as an automated feature on here for those who also want a hard copy of their music list.

Projects is currently empty but will be filled with many wonders and detailed* explanations in the near future. All of my projects are posted on my Github if you're interested.
